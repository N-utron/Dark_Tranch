About this game
The journey begins while you are in the depths of darkness, looking for a cube of light that will bring you out of the terrifying darkness, and try to get away from ghosts and monsters. There is no specific path or maze, but rather an empty place in which there is nothing fixed, and all you have is a pointer that directs you to the nearest thing beside you, whether it is something good or bad. Beware, you do not have attempts or a certain blood level. You will not die or lose in the game, but you may die of fear.
